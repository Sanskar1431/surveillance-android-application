# Student Productivity & Accountability Platform

## Overview

The Student Productivity & Accountability Platform is a comprehensive application designed to help students stay focused, organized, and productive during their study sessions.

The platform combines study management tools, accountability features, and progress tracking into a single application, enabling students to build better study habits and achieve their academic goals.

---

## Features

### 📚 Study Timer

* Pomodoro-based focus sessions
* Custom study durations
* Break reminders
* Session tracking

### ✅ Task Manager

* Create and manage daily tasks
* Set priorities and deadlines
* Mark tasks as completed
* Track productivity over time

### 👥 Study Rooms

* Create private or public study rooms
* Join study sessions with other students
* Collaborative study environment
* Real-time room management

### 📸 Accountability Monitoring

* Periodic screenshot capture during study sessions
* Study activity verification
* Focus tracking and reporting
* User-controlled monitoring settings

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard

* Daily study statistics
* Weekly and monthly reports
* Productivity insights
* Performance visualization

### 🎯 Goal Tracking

* Set study goals
* Monitor progress
* Maintain study streaks
* Achievement tracking
